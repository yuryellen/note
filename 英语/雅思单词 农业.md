Agriculture Article

In the history of agriculture[农业]¹, people have learned to irrigate[灌溉]² their fields using canals and machines such as the tractor[拖拉机]³. Farmers grow cotton[棉花]⁴, garlic[大蒜]⁵, and eggplant[茄子]⁶, while also preparing fodder[饲料]⁷, hay[干草]⁸, and storing them in a haystack[干草堆]⁹. To maintain healthy soil, they remove weed[杂草]¹⁰ and plant crops like sorghum[高粱]¹¹.

Fruit trees grow in the orchard[果园]¹⁹, producing a prolific[多产的]²⁰ harvest when the soil is rich[肥沃的]²¹ and crops become ripe[成熟的]²². The harvest[收获]²³ is then collected and stored for the future.

Some land is arable[适于耕种的]²⁸, while others hold indigenous[当地的]²⁹ plants. To improve yields, farmers use fertiliser[肥料]³⁰, practice careful husbandry[农牧业]³¹, and allow animals to graze[放牧]³². To protect crops,  insecticide[杀虫剂]³⁷ is sprayed, and animals are fed from the trough[饲料槽]³⁸. On a ranch[牧场]³⁹ or plantation[种植场]⁴⁰, workers manage pasture[牧场]⁴¹, keep pigs in the pigpen[猪舍]⁴², and calculate the accumulated temperature of cultivation[栽培积温]⁴³, the accumulated temperature of daytime[昼积温]⁴⁴, and the accumulated temperature of growth[生育积温]⁴⁵. They also study the role of the acellular organism[非细胞生物]⁴⁶ in soil, as well as acid base equilibrium[酸碱平衡]⁴⁷ and acid soil improvement[酸性土壤改良]⁴⁸.


为了保护作物，人们会喷洒杀虫剂³⁷，并从饲料槽³⁸里喂养动物。在牧场³⁹或种植场⁴⁰里，工人们管理牧场⁴¹，把猪养在猪舍⁴²，还要计算栽培积温⁴³、昼积温⁴⁴和生育积温⁴⁵。他们还研究非细胞生物⁴⁶在土壤中的作用，以及酸碱平衡⁴⁷和酸性土壤改良⁴⁸。

Modern farmers rely on agricultural implements[农具]⁴⁹, study agricultural insects[农业昆虫]⁵⁰, and use agricultural machinery[农业机械]⁵¹. Yet, they must also face agricultural pollution[农业污染]⁵², solve every agricultural problem[农业问题]⁵³, and sell both agricultural produce[农产物]⁵⁴ and agricultural products[农产品]⁵⁵ to the market.

现代农民依靠农具⁴⁹，研究农业昆虫⁵⁰，并使用农业机械⁵¹。然而，他们也必须面对农业污染⁵²，解决各种农业问题⁵³，把农产物⁵⁴和农产品⁵⁵卖到市场。
